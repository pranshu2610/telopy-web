.03 How Telopy works?

Telegram is a non-profit, cloud-based instant messaging service. Its BOT feature simplifies the task of sending messages directly from program.

Backend written in Python 3 and its dependencies requests, lxml, beautifulsoup4. Request and Poll used to scrape the notices and process it before sending.

On April end, whole backend was transfered to ML-based automation. Telegram group data are exported occationally to train the model.
